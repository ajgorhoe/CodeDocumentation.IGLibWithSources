Detailed Description

Cartesian and cylindrical parameterization of a cylindrical billiard knot in 3D.

IMPORTANT: This parameterization is WRONG, but it WILL BE KEPT because it produces interesting shapes. The other method, CylindricalBilliardKnot, should be corrected.
