Detailed Description

A 3D parametric curve with bounds - the conical logarithmic spiral.

This conical spiral is based on the Logarithmic spiral r = r(φ) = a * exp(k * φ).

Parameters: a, k, ConicalCurve3DParameterizationFromPolarWithBounds.alpha
