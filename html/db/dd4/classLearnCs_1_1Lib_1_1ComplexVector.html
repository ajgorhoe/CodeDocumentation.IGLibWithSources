static implicit LearnCs.Lib.ComplexVector.operator ComplexVector ( double  array[][])
inlinestatic

Converts array of arrays of double (double[][]) to a complex vector (ComplexVector).

Each component of the outer one-dimensional array is again a 1D array, which is converted to a complex number, thus it must have at most 2 elements (for real and imaginary part), but can have less or be null (see in Complex the operator Complex(double[]).

Parameters
arrayArray of arrays , each of which represents a complex number, and should not have mroe than 2 elements.
