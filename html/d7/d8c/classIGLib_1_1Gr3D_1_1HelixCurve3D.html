Detailed Description

A 3D parametric curve with bounds - a cylindrical helix.

The radius of the helix is a, and its shift (the distance between two consecutive coils) is 2 * π * b (b is sometimes called reduced shift of the helix). The angle of the helix is constant and equal to atan(b/a).

Additional parameters mentioned above: a, b, righthanded
