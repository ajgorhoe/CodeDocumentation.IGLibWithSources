Detailed Description

A Lisajous knot is a knot whose projection on any of the three coordinate planes is a Lissajous curve.

knot cannot be self-intersecting, the three integers n1 , n2 , n3 must be pairwise relatively prime (coprime, GCD=1), and none of the quantities n1*fi2-n2*fi1, n2*fi3-n3*fi2, n3*fi1-n1*fi3 may be an intgerr multiple of π.
