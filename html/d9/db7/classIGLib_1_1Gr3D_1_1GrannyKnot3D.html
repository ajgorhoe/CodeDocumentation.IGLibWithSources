Detailed Description

The granny knot is a composite knot obtained by taking the connected sum of two identical trefoil knots. It is closely related to the square knot, which can also be described as a connected sum of two trefoils. The granny knot and the square knot are the simplest composite knots. Derivative calculation IS provided by this class.
